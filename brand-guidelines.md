# EDELPOP BRAND GUIDELINES

## 1. Brand Overview
* **Brand Name:** Edelpop
* **Tagline:** Besser als Popcorn!
* **Definition:** A premium, light snack alternative designed to deliver a satisfying crunch without the heaviness of traditional snacks.

## 2. Positioning & Essence
* **Category:** Healthy alternative snack.
* **Positioning Statement:** A refined snacking experience — light, airy, and satisfying — without the greasy or heavy after-feel.
* **Key Promise:** A snack that feels better before, during, and after eating.
* **Core Idea:** Light. Crunchy. Clean snacking.

## 3. Brand Personality
Edelpop is:
* **Minimal** (Clean and focused)
* **Playful** (Subtle and sophisticated)
* **Natural** (Real ingredients and colors)
* **Calm** (Not over-stimulating)
* **Modern** (Contemporary aesthetic)

**Edelpop is NOT:** Loud, overly health-focused, aggressive, or clinical.

## 4. Visual Identity System

### Color Palette
| Element | Hex Code | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cream** | `#FFF3E3` | Primary background / Airy feel |
| **Dark Brown** | `#463730` | Primary typography / Grounded |
| **Warm Red** | `#EF442D` | High-impact UI accents / Buttons |

### Typography
* **Headlines:** Montserrat Bold
* **Body Text:** Montserrat Regular or Open Sans
* **Principles:** High legibility, generous kerning, and airy line height.

### Layout Principles
* **Whitespace:** Use generously to emphasize "lightness."
* **Focus:** Maintain one key element per view.
* **Simplicity:** Limit to 2–3 elements per section.
* **Avoid:** Overcrowding, complex decorations, or excessive font variations.

## 5. Flavor & Messaging System

### Messaging Pillars
1.  **Texture:** "Knusprig, dann zart" (Crunchy, then melts in the mouth).
2.  **Clean Snacking:** "Kein fettiges Nachgefühl" (No greasy residue on hands or teeth).
3.  **The Experience:** Focus on the feeling of snacking rather than technical claims.

### Flavor Matrix
| Flavor | Descriptors | Color Accent |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Salt & Pepper** | Knusprig · Salzig · Fein | Slate Grey (`#8E8E8E`) |
| **Cheese & Herbs** | Würzig · Cremig · Mild | Golden Herb (`#D4AF37`) |
| **Caramel** | Zart · Buttrig · Leicht süß | Toasted Caramel (`#B68A5E`) |

* **Composition:** Product pack paired with a bowl; extreme close-up texture shots.
